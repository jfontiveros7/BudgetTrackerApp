## Full Product Description

Budget Tracker App by Konticode Labs is a modern personal finance dashboard built for developers, founders, and digital product sellers who want a budgeting app that already feels intelligent.

Instead of shipping a basic CRUD finance template, this product gives you a more complete experience:

- a polished dark dashboard
- budget and spending visibility
- dynamic dashboard alerts
- AI-ready settings and workflows
- natural-language transaction entry
- searchable recent transactions
- settings for alerts, AI defaults, and reset-to-default controls

It is designed to help you ship faster whether you want to:

- launch a finance SaaS MVP
- sell a dashboard product
- create a client budgeting portal
- extend an AI-assisted admin product
